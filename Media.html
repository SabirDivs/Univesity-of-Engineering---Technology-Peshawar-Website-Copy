Directorate of Media & Publications

The Directorate of Media & Publications was established last year in May 2004, and has been striving to raise UET's profile among key audiences, and build understanding of its unique niche in "higher education". 

The Directorate focuses on promoting research acconplishments, highlighting expertise of faculty, and producing a number of publications, including a UET Newsletter, Annual Report, Department Brochures and other promotional materials.

We also help in developing news articles, and story ideas on faculty research and expertise for print media, assist in media interviews, and ensure media coverage for major events. We provide "still" photography services, and maintain an extensive archive of high-quality stock images of University's activities.

We are committed to enhance University's image by exploiting all possible dissemination techniques. Please feel free to use the Directorate of Media and Publications as your resource for information about the UET.
