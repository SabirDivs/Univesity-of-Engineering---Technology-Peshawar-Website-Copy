Mardan Campus was inaugurated in 2002 with a spirit to bring engineering education to people at their door steps. The campus is spread over an area of more than 22 acres, including a covered area of 190,000 sq .ft., consisting of an administration block, academic blocks, recreation center, students and faculty housing. At Mardan we offer three engineering undergraduate program that include Telecommunication Engineering, Computer Software Engineering and Electrical (Power/Communication) Engineering. All the laboratories have recently been strengthened with the latest cutting-edge equipment. The ever increasing applications demonstrate that student find program offered credible and useful.
Research and development goes side by side – promoting research friendly environment is a key focus of these Departments. The program is formulated to provide the opportunity for students to engage in research based projects. Necessary equipment and software simulation tools, required for accomplishing various research projects, are provided and latest techniques in this regard are also communicated to students. Students are encouraged to pursue research based ideas in their final year projects. The research oriented strategy has proved fruitful in the recent years –some of our graduating engineers had been able to publish the results of their research projects in IEEE sponsored conferences.
